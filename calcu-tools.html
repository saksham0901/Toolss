About Attendance Calculation

Attendance percentage is calculated by dividing the number of days present by the total working days, then multiplying by 100.

Formula:

Attendance Percentage = (Days Present / Total Working Days) × 100

Important Notes:

  • Public holidays are typically not counted as working days
  • Some institutions may have different calculation methods
  • Always verify with your institution's attendance policy
